The Bio of Elon Musk

Timeline of his life so far:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa

  • 1981 - Age 10, Musk starts developing interest in computers while using a Commodore VIC-20

  • 1989 - Starts attending Queen's University

  • 1992 - Starts studying economics and physics as University of Pennsylvania

  • 1999 - Elon creates X.com, a financial services and payment site. It eventually merges with the company Confinity and is now know as PayPal.

  • 2002 - Elon starts Space Exploration Technologies Corp. known as SpaceX, a aerospace manufacturer for advanced space transportation services.

  • 2002 - Elon starts Space Exploration Technologies Corp. known as SpaceX, a aerospace manufacturer for advanced space transportation services. Currently the company is producing reusable staged rockets.

  • 2003 - Elon starts the electric vehicle manufacturing company Tesla. The goal of building affordable and technologically advanced electric powered vehicles.

  • 2006 - Elon invests into SolarCity, a company solar energy company founded by his cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive.

  • 2013 - Musk creates a new transportation initiative called Hyperloop.This was to be a high-speed electric and low pressure tunnel vehicle system that would cut down transport time on land.

  • 2018 - Elon's years of development in SpaceX results in the launch of Falcon Heavy three booster reusable rocket. It successfully launched his own person Tesla car into space.
